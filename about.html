About us

Who we are

The USIU Phoenix Chess Club is a community of passionate chess enthusiasts dedicated to promoting the game across USIU Africa.

Our story

More than a game

The USIU Chess Club is not just about competition. It's a space where individuals come together to share their love for a game that sharpens focus, teaches patience, and builds lasting connections.

We welcome players of all backgrounds — whether you've never touched a piece or you've been playing for years, this is your club.

What drives us

Our mission

To provide a platform for chess enthusiasts to improve their skills, engage in friendly competition, and build lasting connections within the chess community.

♟ Skill development

From beginner fundamentals to advanced opening theory and tournament preparation — we invest in every member's growth.

🏆 Competition

We represent USIU in national events including KUSA and the Kenyan National Chess League. We play to win, but we play with respect.

🤝 Community

Students, faculty, and chess enthusiasts from the wider community are all welcome. Chess has no borders.
