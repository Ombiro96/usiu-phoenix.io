Chess Lessons

Whether you're picking up your first piece or preparing for a national tournament, our curriculum has something for everyone.

What we teach

A real curriculum, not just casual play

Our lessons are structured to build genuine chess understanding — from the first move to complex strategic decisions.

  • Basic rules and fundamentals for absolute beginners
  • Tactics — forks, pins, skewers, discovered attacks
  • Opening principles and popular opening repertoires
  • Tournament preparation and game analysis
  • Effective time management under the clock
Learn and improve

Balanced theory and practice

Our sessions balance theory with practical play. You'll understand the ideas behind the moves, not just memorise lines. Friendly games and simulated tournament scenarios help you apply what you learn immediately.

Strategic play

Think deeper, play better

Master the art of positional play as our instructors walk you through planning, piece coordination, and how to adapt your style to different opponents. Chess is a language — we'll help you become fluent.

Time management

Handle the clock like a pro

Time pressure is where games are won and lost. Our practical exercises train you to make strong decisions quickly — without panicking when the clock is ticking down.
